Shipping & Refund Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

Shipping Policy

Shipping Locations

We currently ship across most PIN codes in India. If your location is not serviceable, you will be notified at checkout.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays are handled on the next working day.

Delivery Time

  • Metro cities: 2–4 working days
  • Other areas: 3–7 working days

Shipping Charges

  • Free shipping for prepaid orders above ₹499
  • ₹50 shipping fee for orders below ₹499
  • Additional ₹30 for Cash on Delivery (COD) orders

Order Tracking

You will receive a tracking link via SMS or email once your order is dispatched.

Delivery Partners

We use trusted logistics partners like Delhivery, Bluedart, and India Post for reliable delivery.

Delivery Delays

We are not liable for delays caused by weather, festivals, strikes, or courier issues. We always aim for timely delivery and will assist if delays occur.

Refund & Return Policy

Returns

Due to the perishable nature of our products (grains, oats, millets), we do not accept returns if the product has been opened or tampered with.

Eligibility for Refunds or Replacements

You are eligible for a refund or replacement if:

  • You received the wrong item
  • The package was damaged during shipping
  • The product was defective or expired upon delivery

How to Request

Email us within 3 days of delivery at support@graina.in with:

  • Your order ID
  • Photos of the product and packaging
  • A brief explanation of the issue

Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days to your original payment method.

Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Opened or used products
  • Requests made after 3 days of delivery
  • Returns due to change of mind or incorrect order placed

Need Help?

Reach out to us anytime at support@graina.in.
