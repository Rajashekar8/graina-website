Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2025

Brand: Graina (by Acinom Retail Traders LLP)

At Graina, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email, phone number (when you fill forms or contact us)
  • Shipping address (during checkout)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, etc.)
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and deliver your orders
  • To respond to your questions or support requests
  • To improve our website and customer experience
  • To send occasional updates, offers, or newsletters (only with your consent)

3. Data Protection

We use SSL encryption and secure servers to store and process your data. Only authorized team members access your information.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with trusted partners (e.g., delivery providers or payment gateways) only to complete your order.

5. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your stored data
  • Ask us to update or delete your personal info
  • Opt out of marketing communications anytime

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Save your cart/session
  • Analyze traffic via tools like Google Analytics

You can control cookies in your browser settings.
